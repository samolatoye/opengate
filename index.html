About Opengate Health Services

Established in 2015 and fully registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria, Opengate Health Services Ltd has been at the forefront of healthcare excellence.

Since our inception, we've been dedicated to training healthcare professionals, strengthening emergency response, advancing neonatal and maternal care, and delivering lifesaving medical evacuation and supply solutions across communities.
